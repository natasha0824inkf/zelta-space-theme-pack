## How to apply

1. Open Slack → workspace name → **Preferences → Themes**
2. Scroll down → **"Or enter a custom theme"**
3. Paste one of the strings above → press **Enter**

Or click **Import** and paste the contents of the matching `.txt` file from the `slack-themes/` folder.
